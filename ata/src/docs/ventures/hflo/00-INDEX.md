## Executive Summary

**HFLO** creates **photorealistic holographic flowers** that bloom, grow, and respond to touch using **solar-powered holographic displays**. Each HFLO unit is a **self-sufficient art piece** requiring no electricity, combining **quantum dot solar cells** (30% efficiency), **holographic projection**, and **AI-generated botanical animations** to deliver living art that lasts 15+ years.

**Market:** $12B global home décor market, targeting eco-conscious millennials/Gen-Z.

**Revenue (Year 3):** $180M (500K units × $360 average price)
