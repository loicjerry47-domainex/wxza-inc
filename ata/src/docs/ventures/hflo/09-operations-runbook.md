## Common Issues

### 1. Dim Hologram

**Cause:** Dusty solar panel or glass  
**Fix:** Clean with microfiber cloth

### 2. Unresponsive Touch

**Cause:** Interference from metal objects  
**Fix:** Move away from metal surfaces

### 3. Battery Not Charging

**Cause:** Solar panel in shadow  
**Fix:** Place near window (2h sunlight/day needed)
