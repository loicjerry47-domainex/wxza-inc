## Executive Summary

**Gcraft** is the world's first **AI-powered gift card marketplace** that uses **ML-driven fraud detection**, **real-time balance verification**, and **instant digital delivery** to create a trusted secondary market for gift cards. Users can **buy discounted gift cards** (5-30% off), **sell unwanted gift cards** (70-85% of value), and **exchange cards** across 500+ brands.

**Market:** $160B global gift card industry, $3B secondary market  
**Revenue (Year 3):** $420M (8.5M transactions × $35 avg. × 14% take rate)

---

## Product Features

### For Buyers
- **Discounted Cards:** 5-30% off face value
- **Instant Delivery:** Digital codes within 60 seconds
- **Balance Guarantee:** 100% balance verification before sale
- **Fraud Protection:** AI detects stolen/counterfeit cards

### For Sellers
- **Fast Cash:** Get 70-85% of card value in 24 hours
- **Bulk Upload:** Sell 100+ cards at once (corporate)
- **Auto-Pricing:** ML suggests optimal price
- **No Listing Fees:** Only pay when card sells

## Technical Highlights

- **Fraud Detection:** 99.7% accuracy (gradient boosting, 50K features)
- **Balance Verification:** API integration with 500+ retailers
- **Instant Settlement:** Stripe Connect (sub-second payouts)
- **Mobile Apps:** iOS, Android (React Native)
- **Transaction Volume:** 15K/day peak (holiday season)
