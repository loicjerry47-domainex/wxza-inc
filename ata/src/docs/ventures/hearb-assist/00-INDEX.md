## Executive Summary

**HEARb ASSIST** is a comprehensive **AI-powered assistive technology platform** combining **real-time speech-to-text**, **text-to-speech**, **visual assistance** (object recognition, scene description), and **smart home integration** to empower **466 million people globally** with hearing, vision, or mobility impairments.

**Market:** $26B global assistive technology market  
**Revenue (Year 3):** $280M (1.2M users × $19/month × 12 months)

## Features

### Hearing Assistance
- **Real-Time Captioning:** <200ms latency (95 languages)
- **Sound Recognition:** 100+ sounds (doorbell, alarm, crying baby)
- **Sign Language Translation:** ASL ↔ English (95% accuracy)

### Vision Assistance
- **Object Recognition:** 10K objects, 97% accuracy
- **Scene Description:** AI narrates surroundings
- **Text Reader:** OCR + TTS (150 languages)
- **Navigation:** Turn-by-turn audio directions

### Smart Home
- **Voice Control:** Control lights, locks, thermostats
- **Visual Alerts:** Flash lights for sounds (doorbell → lights blink)
- **Emergency Assistance:** One-touch SOS

## Technical Highlights

- **AI Models:** 12 on-device models (privacy-first)
